About Me

Hello! I'm Zee — a developer, and lifelong learner dedicated to making programming accessible. I believe everyone should be able to build tools, solve problems, and understand the logic behind the apps and systems we use every day.

Through hands-on projects and structured Q&A lessons, I’m here to help you go from “I’ve never coded before” to “I just built my first app!”

About Learn Programming With Zee

Learn Programming With Zee is an independent digital learning initiative built for clarity, curiosity, and confidence. It is not a registered business or institution—just a community-focused project aiming to make programming easy and accessible for everyone.

Instead of long tutorials, we focus on short, practical questions and answers—so you can learn exactly what you need, when you need it. The platform is designed around real-life analogies, visual explanations, and clean, simple steps. No jargon, no fluff—just programming that makes sense.
